introduction

N cube resturant was founded by Late Thiru. K.S. Thirupathi Raja founder of Guru Sweets in Rajapalayam, Srinivasa Sweets in Bangalore and Sri Ananda Bhavan in Washermenpet, Chennai. it is a chain of vegetarian and non vegetarians restaurants and confectioners headquartered in Adyar, Chennai, India. It is the oldest such chain in Chennai and has 140 outlets across India.
